Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Do’s
	•	Create strong and unique passwords for each account
	•	Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever available
	•	Always check website links before entering personal or banking details
	•	Keep your mobile, apps, and software updated regularly
	•	Use only secure and trusted internet connections
	•	Monitor your bank statements and transactions frequently
	•	Download applications only from official stores (Play Store/App Store)
	•	Report any suspicious activity immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930

 Don’ts
	•	Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
	•	Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
	•	Do not trust calls pretending to be from banks asking for details
	•	Avoid storing sensitive data on public or shared devices
	•	Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
	•	Avoid opening attachments from unknown emails
	•	Do not ignore unusual account notifications or alerts
	•	Do not respond to fake job offers or prize-winning messages

 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
	•	Always confirm the receiver’s name before sending money
	•	Do not approve unknown or suspicious UPI collect requests
	•	Avoid scanning random QR codes, especially for receiving money
	•	Use only official and trusted banking apps for transactions

Report Cybercrime
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
